WHAT IS THINKING TOMORROW ?

Thinking Tomorrow is a non-profitable organisation that works continuously to transform the present days world with the use of technology . We beleive technology is a tool that can revolutionize the present world if used extensively. We try to give radiance of technology in every human's life. However, that does not advocate the triumph of technologial resources over Human Resources. Human was, is and will always be the master of whatever technological resources present in the world and these resources will just help us in our daily life. This is what we try to achieve by integration of technology into every human's life .

We are Sajjad Ahmed and Hrishikesh Bhanja of Don bosco School, Park Circus. Our job is to tranform this world into a better place through technology. Visit our GitHub page to know more.
